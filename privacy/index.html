BreakThru

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

BreakThru is a voice-powered focus app that turns brain dumps into one task at a time. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Sign in with Apple may provide an identifier, name, and email address depending on your Apple settings.
  • Voice recordings and text brain dumps: Used to transcribe and extract tasks.
  • Tasks, pledges, reminders, and completion data: Used to run the focus queue, check-ins, and progress features.
  • Device tokens: Used only if you enable push notifications.
  • Basic diagnostics: Used to keep the app reliable and understand core app functionality.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate your account.
  • Transcribe brain dumps and extract tasks.
  • Prioritize tasks and show one task at a time.
  • Send reminders and check-ins when enabled.
  • Maintain, debug, and improve the app.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple: Sign in with Apple authentication.
  • OpenAI: Voice transcription and task extraction.
  • Supabase: Authentication, database, storage, and backend functions.
  • Firebase: Push notification infrastructure and app reliability tooling.

Tracking

BreakThru does not sell your personal data. The first App Store release does not use your data for cross-app tracking.

Data Retention And Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can initiate account deletion in the app from Settings. Deleting your account removes your account, tasks, recordings, pledges, reminders, and settings from BreakThru systems unless we are legally required to retain something.
