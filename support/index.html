Support

Need help with BreakThru? Email bilal@divinelightlabs.com and we will get back to you.

How do I sign in?

Use Sign in with Apple from the welcome screen.

How do I create tasks?

Record a voice brain dump or type it in. BreakThru extracts your tasks and shows one focus card at a time.

How do reminders and check-ins work?

When notifications are on, BreakThru sends reminders and check-ins for the task you committed to. You can adjust how often it checks in, and set quiet hours, in Settings.

How do I delete my account?

Open BreakThru, go to Settings, and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your account and associated BreakThru data.
