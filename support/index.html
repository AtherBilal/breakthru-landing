How do I sign in?

Use Sign in with Apple from the welcome screen.

How do I create tasks?

Record a voice brain dump or use text input. BreakThru extracts tasks and shows one focus card at a time.

How do reminders work?

If notifications are enabled, BreakThru can send push reminders and check-ins for committed tasks. You can adjust reminder intensity and quiet hours in Settings.

Does the first release include SMS or iMessage?

No. The first App Store release uses in-app state and push reminders. SMS/iMessage accountability is not part of this initial public build.

How do I delete my account?

Open BreakThru, go to Settings, and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your account and associated BreakThru data.
